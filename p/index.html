Meet Udon: The Noodle of Chaos and Cuddles!

Straight from the finest puppy rescue boutique (a.k.a. MSRPAC, formerly Riverside Humane Society), Udon is a one-of-a-kind American Pit Bull Terrier + Mystery Mutt mix guaranteed to steal your heart—and your snacks.

At a sophisticated age of 2 months and 3 days when adopted, Udon was already an expert in naps, cuddles, and treat negotiations. Now, he continues to refine these skills with unwavering dedication.

Sporting a stylish brown coat with designer white patches, he’s a certified good boy with a signature one-ear-forward, one-ear-back look that screams “I’m both wise and ridiculous.”

Whether he’s tripping over his own paws or perfecting his sad puppy face for maximum cuddles, Udon is here to bring laughter, chaos, and unconditional love.

Neutered but still full of sass, Udon is here to remind you that the best things in life are rescued, loved, and slightly lopsided. Adopt, don’t shop!
