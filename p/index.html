Meet Udon: The Noodle-Legged Goofball!

Adopted from the MSRPC, Udon is a two-month-old American Pit Bull Terrier x Mystery Mutt mix with more personality than he has coordination. He’s brown with white patches, but his true defining feature? One ear flops forward, the other flops back—because symmetry is overrated.

Neutered, opinionated, and powered by puppy zoomies, Udon’s life mission includes:
✔️ Chewing things he absolutely should not chew.
✔️ Mastering the art of the dramatic flop.
✔️ Using his big, soulful eyes to get extra treats (it always works).

Whether he’s tripping over his own paws or perfecting his sad puppy face for maximum cuddles, Udon is here to bring laughter, chaos, and unconditional love.
